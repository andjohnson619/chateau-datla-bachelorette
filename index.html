Know Before You Go

Logistics

Flights

Fly into SFO (San Francisco, ~1 hr drive), OAK (Oakland, ~1.5 hr drive), or STS (Sonoma County, closest option).

SFO has the most flight options. STS is the most convenient if you can find a route.

Lodging

House is booked. Check out the listing:

View the Airbnb Listing

Getting There

We will coordinate carpools and Ubers from the airport.

Share your arrival time in the group chat so we can match up rides. Once in Sonoma, we will have cars for getting around.

Weather

Sonoma in late June: 75-90°F days, 50-60°F nights.

Dry and sunny. Bring layers for cooler evenings and comfortable shoes for walking through vineyards and hiking.
